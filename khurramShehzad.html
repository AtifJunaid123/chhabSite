About Our School

Govt. Boys High School Chhab is dedicated to providing excellent education to nurture young minds for a successful future. Established in 1980, our school prides itself on creating well-rounded individuals who excel academically and socially.

  • Experienced & passionate faculty
  • State-of-the-art library and science labs
  • Modern sports and playground facilities
  • Focus on moral values and leadership skills

Principal's Message

"At GBHS Chhab, we strive for excellence not just in academics, but also in character and values. Our goal is to inspire holistic growth, confidence and a lifelong love of learning in every student. We welcome you to our family."
- Imran Khan, Principal
